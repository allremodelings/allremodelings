Our Story

Meet Fernando Torres

All Remodeling and Painting RG LLC was built on a simple but powerful commitment: deliver the quality of work you'd want in your own home. Founded by Fernando Torres, the company has grown into one of Tampa Bay's most trusted names in residential remodeling and painting.

Fernando brings hands-on expertise to every project — personally overseeing quality control and maintaining direct communication with every client. His philosophy is straightforward: do the job right, be honest about timelines and pricing, and never cut corners.

Every project we take on carries our name and our reputation. That means we don't leave until the work is done to a standard we're proud to stand behind.

"We built this company on one promise: every home we touch will be treated like our own. Insurance, experience, and quality finishes — every single time."
